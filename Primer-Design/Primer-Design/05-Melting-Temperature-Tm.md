# Melting Temperature (Tm)

## What is Tm?

Melting Temperature (Tm) is the temperature at which 50% of the DNA duplex becomes single-stranded.

## Ideal Tm

- Between 55°C and 65°C
- The forward and reverse primers should have similar Tm values.

## Why is Tm important?

- Ensures efficient primer binding
- Improves PCR specificity
- Reduces non-specific amplification
