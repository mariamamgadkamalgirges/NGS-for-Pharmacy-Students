# Overlap-Layout-Consensus (OLC)

## What is OLC?

OLC identifies overlaps between sequencing reads and reconstructs the genome.

## Best Used For

- Long-read sequencing
- PacBio
- Oxford Nanopore

## Advantages

- Better handling of repetitive regions
- High assembly accuracy

## Limitations

- Slow
- Computationally intensive
- Requires high sequencing coverage
