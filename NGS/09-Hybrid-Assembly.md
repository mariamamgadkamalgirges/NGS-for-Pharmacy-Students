# Hybrid Assembly

## What is Hybrid Assembly?

Hybrid assembly combines short-read and long-read sequencing data to improve genome assembly quality.

## Why Use Hybrid Assembly?

- Resolves repetitive regions
- Improves assembly accuracy
- Produces longer contigs

## Advantages

- High-quality assemblies
- Combines the strengths of different sequencing technologies

## Limitations

- Requires multiple sequencing datasets
- More computational resources
