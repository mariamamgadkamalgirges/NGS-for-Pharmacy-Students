# Clinical Applications of PCR

## Infectious Diseases
PCR is used to detect viruses, bacteria, and parasites.

Examples:
- COVID-19
- HIV
- Hepatitis B
- Tuberculosis

## Oncology
PCR detects cancer-related mutations.

## Pharmacogenomics
PCR helps identify genetic variations that affect drug response.

## Personalized Medicine
PCR supports treatment decisions based on genetic information.
