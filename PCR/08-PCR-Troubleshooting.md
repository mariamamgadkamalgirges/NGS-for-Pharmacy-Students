# PCR Troubleshooting

## No PCR Product
Possible causes:
- Poor primer design
- Low DNA concentration
- Incorrect PCR conditions

## Multiple Bands
Possible causes:
- Non-specific primer binding
- Low annealing temperature

## Smearing
Possible causes:
- Degraded DNA
- Too many PCR cycles

## Contamination
Always use sterile pipette tips and include a negative control.
