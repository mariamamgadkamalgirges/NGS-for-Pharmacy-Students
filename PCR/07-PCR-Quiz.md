# PCR Quiz

## Question 1
What does PCR stand for?

Answer:
Polymerase Chain Reaction.

---

## Question 2
Which enzyme is used in PCR?

Answer:
Taq DNA Polymerase.

---

## Question 3
What are the three main PCR steps?

Answer:
1. Denaturation
2. Annealing
3. Extension

---

## Question 4
How many cycles are usually performed?

Answer:
25–35 cycles.
