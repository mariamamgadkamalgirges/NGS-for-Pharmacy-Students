# Binning

## What is Binning?

Binning groups contigs into clusters that belong to the same organism.

## Why is Binning Important?

- Reconstruct microbial genomes
- Reduce data complexity
- Improve taxonomic classification

## Common Software

- MaxBin
- CONCOCT

## Advantages

- Genome reconstruction
- Better taxonomic resolution

## Limitations

- May produce incomplete genomes
- Accuracy depends on sequencing quality
