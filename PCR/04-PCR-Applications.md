# PCR Applications

## Clinical Diagnosis
PCR is used to detect infectious diseases such as COVID-19, tuberculosis, and hepatitis.

## Genetic Testing
PCR helps identify inherited genetic disorders.

## Cancer Research
PCR is used to detect gene mutations associated with cancer.

## Forensic Science
PCR is used for DNA fingerprinting and criminal investigations.

## Pharmaceutical Research
PCR is used in drug discovery, pharmacogenomics, and personalized medicine.
